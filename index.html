WF Planning Parameters

Input Metrics

Volume

The total number of units, tasks, or transactions handled within a specific period.

Time Effort

The total amount of time required to complete a task or process, usually measured in hours.

Number of Workdays

The total number of days employees are scheduled to work within a specific period.

Daily Working Hours

The number of hours employees are expected to work in a single day.

Operating Hours

The total number of hours the business operates within a specific period (e.g., daily, weekly).

Shrinkage (%)

The percentage of time lost due to factors like breaks, meetings, and other non-productive activities.

Current HC (Headcount)

The total number of employees currently employed in a specific department or role.

Cost per Hour (Labor Cost)

The hourly rate paid to employees, including wages, benefits, and other related expenses.

Revenue per Hour

The amount of revenue generated by each employee per hour of work.

Output Metrics

Staffing Position

The optimal number of employees needed to meet the workload demand efficiently. This metric will also indicate whether you are overstaffed or understaffed.

Direct Cost

The total cost associated with employing the required staff, including wages, benefits, and other expenses.

Revenue

The total revenue generated by the workforce within a specific period.

Direct Margin

The difference between the total revenue and the direct cost, representing the profitability of the workforce. Example: If your Direct Margin is 37%, it means that for every dollar of revenue, 37 cents remain after covering direct costs.
